About BatReviewer
BatReviewer is a lightweight tool for browsing, listening to, and organising field recordings of bat echolocation calls. It enables rapid visualisation of waveforms and spectrograms, basic signal inspection, file copying, and heterodyne audio preview for quick in-field analyses.
